## 1. Vision & The Problem

Digital payments were growing rapidly, but merchant onboarding and compliance processes remained fragmented, manual, and difficult for SMBs to navigate. Many merchants struggled with delayed activation, failed verification checks, and unclear payment workflows.

1. **Complex Compliance Requirements:** PSD2, KYC, AML, and SCA obligations created friction during onboarding and transaction approval flows.
2. **Operational Inefficiencies:** Manual review processes and incomplete onboarding journeys increased drop-offs and delayed merchant activation.
3. **Payment Reliability Risks:** Failed Faster Payments and transaction monitoring gaps created revenue leakage and operational escalations.
4. **The Goal:** Support the creation of seamless, compliant onboarding and payment operations that balanced regulatory requirements with merchant experience.

## 2. The Solution: Compliance-Led Payment Operations

I supported Product, Compliance, and Risk teams in improving onboarding workflows, payment monitoring, and operational efficiency across merchant payment journeys.

### The Digital Core: Merchant Onboarding & Payments

1. **Onboarding Optimisation:** Supported onboarding improvements for 50+ SMB merchants, contributing to a 20% increase in activation completion.
2. **Compliance-by-Design:** Helped translate PSD2, KYC, AML, and SCA requirements into 15+ actionable product user stories.
3. **SQL-Based Funnel Analysis:** Used SQL reporting and onboarding funnel analysis to identify friction points and optimise user progression.
4. **Payment Operations Support:** Assisted in investigating Faster Payments failures and transaction exceptions to minimise disruption.

### The Operational Extension: Risk & Compliance Collaboration

1. **CDD & Sanctions Monitoring:** Worked alongside Compliance and Risk teams to support customer due diligence, sanctions screening, and monitoring flows for £1M+ payments.
2. **Cross-functional Coordination:** Partnered with QA, Compliance, Product, and Operations teams to reduce release inefficiencies and improve workflow clarity.

## 3. Fintech, Payments & Compliance

A key focus of my work was ensuring that compliance requirements strengthened trust and reliability instead of creating operational bottlenecks.

1. **Merchant Activation Improvements:** Funnel analysis and onboarding optimisation improved onboarding completion rates by 12–20%.
2. **Compliance Translation:** Helped convert complex PSD2, AML, KYC, and SCA requirements into structured product workflows, reducing QA rework by 25%.
3. **Payment Reliability:** Investigated Faster Payments logs and payment failures, helping prevent £500K+ in potential revenue impact.
4. **Risk Monitoring Support:** Contributed to customer due diligence and transaction monitoring processes supporting £1M+ payment operations.

## 4. Key Product Decisions

The following operational and product decisions shaped the effectiveness of onboarding and compliance workflows:

1. **Decision 1 — Compliance Embedded into Product Flows:** Rather than treating compliance as a separate operational layer, onboarding journeys integrated KYC, AML, and SCA requirements directly into the merchant experience to reduce friction and confusion.
2. **Decision 2 — Funnel Analysis before Redesign:** Before suggesting onboarding changes, I used SQL analysis and funnel reporting to identify where merchants were dropping off. This ensured decisions were based on behavioural data rather than assumptions.
3. **Decision 3 — Prioritising High-Impact Friction Points:** Instead of redesigning the entire onboarding process, we focused first on the highest drop-off stages that impacted merchant activation speed and operational workload.
4. **Decision 4 — Risk & Product Alignment:** Compliance and Risk teams were involved early in workflow discussions so that onboarding journeys remained regulatorily compliant while still being operationally efficient.
5. **Decision 5 — Faster Payments Monitoring as a Revenue Protection Layer:** Payment failure investigations were prioritised not just as operational support tickets, but as a mechanism to protect merchant trust and reduce revenue leakage.
6. **Decision 6 — Structured User Stories for Regulatory Clarity:** Breaking regulatory requirements into clear user stories improved coordination between Product, QA, and Compliance teams, reducing ambiguity during releases.
7. **Decision 7 — Learning Through Cross-functional Exposure:** As an intern, I intentionally worked closely across Product, Compliance, Risk, and Operations to accelerate learning and build a practical understanding of payment ecosystems quickly.

## 5. Reflections & Stack

### What I Learned

1. **Compliance is a Product Experience:** Regulatory requirements directly impact user trust and onboarding success when poorly implemented.
2. **Data Drives Better Product Decisions:** SQL-based funnel analysis made it easier to identify operational bottlenecks objectively.
3. **Payments Require Operational Precision:** Small failures in payment workflows can quickly scale into major merchant trust and revenue issues.
4. **Cross-functional Learning Accelerates Growth:** Working closely with Compliance, Risk, QA, and Product teams helped me rapidly understand fintech operations end-to-end.

### What I'd Do Differently

1. **Earlier Instrumentation:** I would introduce more detailed onboarding analytics earlier to improve visibility into user drop-offs.
2. **Automation of Monitoring Workflows:** Some operational review processes could have been automated earlier to reduce manual dependency.
3. **Merchant Feedback Loops:** Structured merchant interviews during onboarding would have provided stronger qualitative insights alongside SQL analysis.
